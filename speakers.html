He has successfully implemented many projects with advanced security solutions to protect sensitive data and enhance organizational resilience against cybersecurity threats. He holds a number of vendor certifications, most notably Cisco Certified Internetworking Expert (CCIE) 41436. Jody strives to enable organizations to prosper, driving best practices in network security and finding the best balancing point between empowerment and vigilance.
