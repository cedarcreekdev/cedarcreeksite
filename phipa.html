The Role of PHIPA

PHIPA provides a set of rules for:

Consent - Ensuring that patients' consent is obtained for the collection, use, and disclosure of their personal health information.

Maintaining Confidentiality and Security - Safeguarding personal health information through secure practices.

Digital Security - Implementing robust networks, firewalls, and computer systems to protect healthcare data.

Accessing and Updating Information - Managing access and updates to personal health information in a secure manner.

Fundraising and Marketing - Establishing guidelines for using personal health information in fundraising and marketing.

Accountability and Complaints - Addressing complaints and inaccuracies in personal health information.

Breach Management - Handling breaches to the legislation effectively and efficiently.
