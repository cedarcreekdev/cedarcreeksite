Security Services

Keeping Your Data Safe

  • PHIPA security and privacy compliance
  • Vulnerability assessments / audits
  • Encryption
  • Firewall implementation & maintenance

At Cedarcreek Networking, we understand the importance of protecting your sensitive data. Our comprehensive suite of security services is designed to keep your data safe from threats and ensure compliance with industry regulations. Explore our key services below to learn how we can help you safeguard your valuable information.


PHIPA Security and Privacy Compliance
We specialize in helping healthcare organizations achieve compliance with the Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA). Our tailored solutions ensure that your organization meets the strict security and privacy requirements outlined in PHIPA, protecting patient confidentiality and maintaining trust.

Vulnerability Assessments / Audits
Identify and address potential security weaknesses in your IT infrastructure with our vulnerability assessments and audits. Our experienced security professionals conduct thorough assessments to pinpoint vulnerabilities and provide actionable recommendations to strengthen your defenses.

Encryption
Protect your sensitive data from unauthorized access with encryption solutions from Cedarcreek Networking. We implement robust encryption techniques to secure data at rest and in transit, ensuring that only authorized users can access and decipher your confidential information.

Firewall Implementation & Maintenance
Firewalls are essential for protecting your network from external threats. We design, implement, and maintain firewall solutions tailored to your specific needs, providing robust protection against unauthorized access, malware, and other cyber threats.

Intrusion Detection / Penetration Testing
Detect and mitigate security breaches before they cause harm to your organization with our intrusion detection and penetration testing services. Our security experts employ advanced techniques to identify and address vulnerabilities in your network, helping you stay one step ahead of cyber threats.

Virus Protection
Protect your endpoints from malware, viruses, and other malicious threats with our comprehensive virus protection solutions. We deploy advanced antivirus software and implement proactive security measures to keep your systems safe and secure.

PCI Compliance
If your organization processes payment card transactions, achieving PCI compliance is essential for maintaining trust and avoiding costly penalties. We help you navigate the complex requirements of the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) and implement the necessary security measures to protect cardholder data.


At Cedarcreek Networking, we are committed to keeping your data safe and secure. Trust us to provide the expertise and resources you need to protect your organization from cyber threats and maintain compliance with industry regulations. Contact us today to learn more about our security services and how we can help you safeguard your data.
